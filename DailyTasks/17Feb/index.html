Target attribute

tells where to display the linked url

  • _self: {default} open in same frame/window as current page. Google
  • _parent: opens in parent of current frame/window Google
  • _top: open in current window without any frame Google
  • framename: open in specified frame Google
  • _blank: open in new unamed window or tab Google
  • blank Google
