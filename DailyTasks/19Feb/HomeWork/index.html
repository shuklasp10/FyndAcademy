difference in del and strike tag

  • del tag is used to represent text deleted from document
  • strike(deprecated) or s tag is used to represent no longer accurate or relevant text in document
  • del offers two additional attributes
    • datetime: tells date and time at which text was deleted. format is YYYY-MM-DDThh:mm:ssTZD
    • cite: url value, links to a document which explain why text was deleted
