Nested iFrame

Sandbox Attribute

Sandbox is used to put restrictions on content

Values of sandbox are:

allow-downloads
allow download files
allow-download-without-user-activation
allow-forms
allow-modals
allow-orientation-lock
allow-pointer-lock
allow-popups
allow-popups-to-escape-sandbox
allow-presentation
allow-same-origin
allow-scripts
allow-storage-access-by-user-activation
allow-top-navigation
allow-top-navigation-by-user-activation
allow-top-navigation-to-custom-protocols

margin width

  • marginwidth is used to give margin left and right of content in iFrame element. Not supported in HTML5
  • marginheight is used to give top and bottom margin to content in iframe
